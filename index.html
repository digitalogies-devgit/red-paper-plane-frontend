06. Uploading your own video to our video brochures

Uploading your own video to our video brochures is a very easy process.

  • 1. Plug the supplied USB cord into the video brochure
  • 2. Plug the USB cord into your computer
  • 3. The video brochure will launch as an external hard drive
  • 4. Locate the test video on the video brochure and delete it
  • 5. Be sure to empty your trash especially on a Mac to free up space
  • 6. Drag your new video into the same location as the test video was
  • 7. Eject the video brochure as you would an external hard drive.
  • 8. Play your video to be sure everything looks good.

Please Note: The 2.4" Video Brochures require a special conversion to the video file in order to play on the unit. It is highly recommended that we do the upload for these. If you need us to convert your video file please reach out to your account manager and they can assist in the conversion of your file. Allow 24-48 hours to convert.

09. Video Brochure Batteries

Complete information regarding the video brochure batteries.

  • What kind are they?

    Polymer Lithium Ion - Single Battery with less than or equal to 2 cells

  • What is the stand-by time?

    4-6 Months

  • What is the Lifespan?

    Batteries can be recharged 300-400 times. If batteries are left with no charge it will degrade the amount of charge the battery can hold and reduce the play time.

  • What are the standard size of batteries used?

    2.4” Video Screen -3.7V 1.48Wh (400mah). Battery play time is 1.5hrs
    4.3” Video Screen – 3.7V 2.22Wh (600mah). Battery play time is 1.5hrs
    7” Video Screen – 3.7V 4.44Wh (1200mah). Battery play time is 1.5hrs
    10.1” Video Screen – 3.7V 5.55Wh (1500mah). Battery play time is 1.5hrs

  • Can you ship and/or mail video brochures?

    Yes, you can do both without any specific labeling if you are sending 2 or less video brochures within the shipment since the batteries are contained within the video brochures and they have less than 4 cells and are under 20Wh. If you have any further questions please consult with your carrier rep or USPS for specific guidelines.

Please Note:

Video brochure batteries need to be charged as soon as the battery power has been depleted. The battery will become damaged and not hold a charge if kept empty too long. Like most electronic products, batteries need to be charged when empty.

We cannot guarantee how much charge a battery can hold after 3 months as this depends on the environment the video brochures are stored in and how long the video brochure was stored with a depleted battery. We do not warranty and video brochure battery after 3 months from purchase date.
